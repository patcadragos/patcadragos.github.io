Project Overview

The Autonomous Polishing Assembly is a real-life, production-ready CNC project that combines mechanical engineering, precision manufacturing, and integrated electrical control systems. Designed in SolidWorks and manufactured through CNC precision machining, this machine is currently in active industrial use.

SolidWorks CNC Machining Motovario Motor Bosch Profiles Custom Bracket Electrical Control

Mechanical Design

Structure & Framework

Material: Industrial Bosch profile system provides a rigid, modular foundation for the entire assembly. This choice ensures precision alignment and long-term durability in production environments.

Support System: The machine features a custom support structure made from 4 Bosch profiles and an aluminum mounting plate. This aluminum plate serves as the structural backbone for all rotating and electrical components.

Motor Mounting Solution

One of the key engineering challenges was mounting the Motovario motor with precision. Standard off-the-shelf mounting solutions were incompatible with our design constraints. Solution: A custom two-piece bracket was designed in SolidWorks and manufactured on a CNC machine. This custom bracket ensures perfect motor alignment and load distribution.

Polishing Mechanism

Magnetic Disc System: A custom-engineered disc rotates with the motor shaft. The disc features 8 strategically placed neodymium magnets arranged for optimal polishing action.

Steel Filing Polishing: Inside the universal plastic box sits a working mass of steel filings. As the magnetic disc rotates above, the filings are agitated and lifted by the magnetic field, providing a uniform polishing action on non-magnetic workpieces inside the box.

Non-Magnetic Design Rationale: This machine is specifically designed for polishing non-magnetic pieces. The magnetic system ensures consistent material distribution and eliminates the need for mechanical brushes or abrading surfaces.

CNC Manufacturing Process

From CAD to Production

  • 3D Modeling in SolidWorks: Precision modeling of all custom components, including detailed motor models and mounting brackets
  • CNC Machining: All custom parts — brackets, disc, and specialized components — were manufactured on CNC equipment with tight tolerances
  • Quality Assurance: Each part verified for dimensional accuracy before assembly
  • Manual Assembly: Final assembly completed on-site with full integration testing

Custom Components Manufactured

  • Two-piece motor mounting bracket (precision aluminum)
  • Magnetic polishing disc with integrated magnet pockets
  • Support plate with motor mounting interface
  • Electrical enclosure mounting brackets

Electrical Control Systems

Control Panel Features

A custom electrical control panel integrates all safety and operational systems:

  • Dual Latching Buttons: Two momentary buttons that activate and deactivate each other automatically. When one button is pressed, the other is released — preventing simultaneous operation and ensuring safe machine control.
  • Emergency Stop Button: A rotary emergency stop (E-Stop) button for immediate machine shutdown in case of anomalies or safety concerns.
  • Safety Interlocks: Integrated electrical logic ensures proper operation sequences and prevents unsafe conditions.
  • Motor Driver Integration: Robust control circuitry for reliable motor operation under full load.

Operational Modes

  • ON Mode: Press and hold button — motor runs continuously with full torque
  • OFF Mode: Press alternate button — motor stops, any residual rotation dampens
  • Emergency Stop: Rotate E-Stop — immediate power cutoff
